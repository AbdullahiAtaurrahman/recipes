Hot Dog Casserole

Description

This hot dog casserole is a childhood favorite, and I haven't run across another one like it. Definitely a tasty, unique flavor unlike any other.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package rotini pasta
  • Â¼ cup butter
  • 2 sweet onions, chopped
  • 1 (26 ounce) can tomato soup
  • 1 (16 ounce) package hot dogs, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, undrained
  • 4 teaspoons dill pickle relish
  • 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil; cook rotini at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; drain.
  3. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onions until translucent, 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Transfer onions to a large bowl and mix in pasta, tomato soup, hot dogs, corn with juice, dill pickle relish, and yellow mustard.
  5. Pour mixture into a baking dish.
  6. Pour mixture into a baking dish
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbling and top is lightly browned, about 1 hour.
